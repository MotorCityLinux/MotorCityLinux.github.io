About!


We are a non-profit, open source based organization established to create custom linux solutions for non-profit organizations in the Greater Detroit Metropolitan Area. When we say open source based organization I mean that the commits and contributors are the only things of which the organization is comprised. Of course, anyone can contribute to the projects, organization as a whole, or this very webpage. All materials and documents are to be hosted on this Organization's GitHub account within the various repos.


Customized Linux Solutions? By that we mean linux installations and live enviroments that are tailored for your specific needs. We can work with anything from standard software to unique content payloads.

We are a non-profit organization because generating profit is not end aligned with the goals of assisting non-profit organizations. Also, we are not smashing atoms here, we are establishing an easy way for non-profit organizations to work with linux.

Motor City Linux, is not exclusively aligned with any other organization. As we said, it is what it is made of. Currently most of the contributors are affilated with Motor City Free Geek, because they are also doing some awesome things.
